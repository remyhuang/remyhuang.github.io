The goal of music thumbnailing is to find a short, continuous segment of a song that represents the whole song that also known as Music Highlight Extraction. Let us show eight pop songs for demo! (we can extract any number of seconds of music segments by our proposed method and in this demo we show the most representative 30-seconds)
